About Our Business

Quality craftsmanship and personal service since 2010.

Our Story

Mountain Stitch Sewing Co. was founded with a passion for creating beautiful, durable sewing projects and helping customers keep their favorite clothing looking its best.

What started as a small home-based sewing business has grown into a trusted local company serving customers throughout North Georgia.

Our Mission

We believe every stitch matters. Our goal is to provide high-quality alterations, embroidery, and custom sewing services while delivering exceptional customer service.
